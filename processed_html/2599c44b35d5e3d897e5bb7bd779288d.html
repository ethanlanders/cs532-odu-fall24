Agricultural assistance is key to feed hungry people in war zones: FAO Agricultural assistance is key to feed hungry people in war zones: FAO 11 September 2024 Download Amid the brutal war in Gaza and fears of famine, the Food and Agriculture Organization ( FAO ) is providing Palestinian livestock holders with veterinary kits to keep their animals healthy and disease-free. This is just one example of the UN agency’s work to ensure food security and economic stability in conflict zones, including the West Bank, Ukraine and Sudan.
