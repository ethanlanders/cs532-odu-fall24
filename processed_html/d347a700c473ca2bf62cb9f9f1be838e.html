Content International Parliamentary Scholarship (IPS) The German Bundestag, in cooperation with Freie Universität, Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin and Technische Universität Berlin, awards up to 120 scholarships each year to young high school graduates from 50 countries. With the International Parliamentary Scholarship (IPS) of the German Bundestag, you can get to know parliamentary work in detail for five months and, among other things, experience a three-month internship in a Member of Parliament’s office. Application deadline: 31 August 2024
