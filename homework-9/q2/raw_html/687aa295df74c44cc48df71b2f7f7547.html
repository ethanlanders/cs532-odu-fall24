Middle East

The Israeli-Palestinian conflict is something that is reflecting globally in the streets of the capitals of Europe, in the United States, in Australia and definitely in the region. We are playing a poker game with a young generation that without any perspective on solutions, can find other ways to get an outlet for their frustration. That is very dangerous, and it's dangerous for all.

Tor Wennesland, outgoing Special Coordinator for the Middle East Peace Process, in an exclusive interview with UN News
