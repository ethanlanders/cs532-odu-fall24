Ellmer College of Health Sciences Ellmer College of Health Sciences Learn More New Building Dedication Old Dominion University hosted an opening celebration, including a ribbon cutting, for the Ellmer College of Health Sciences’ new home on Aug. 29. The recently-completed 128,257-square-foot building is home to two public-facing clinics, the Faschini Wallach Center for Restorative Therapies, the Sofia & David Konikoff Dental Hygiene Care Facility, and six of the University’s health sciences programs.
