International Maritime, Ports & Logistics Institute International Maritime, Ports & Logistics Institute Why Maritime at ODU? ODU's proximity to the Port of Virginia and its thriving maritime industry makes it an ideal place to study maritime and supply chain management. It is the only university in the United States that offers a "Maritime and Supply Chain Management" undergraduate major and one of only two universities to offer a "Maritime" undergraduate major.
