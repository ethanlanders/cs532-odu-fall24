About Us The Computer Science Graduate Society (CSGS) at Old Dominion University welcomes you! The purpose of CSGS is to enhance the academic, professional and social life of Computer Science Master's and Doctoral students, as well as all ODU students who are interested in learning more about the Computer Science field. You are welcome to join us in our Tea Time meetings, Coffee Hour meetings, Special events and Workshops, learning about this wide-range subject that connects to every other field! Contact us at: CSGradSociety@odu.edu
